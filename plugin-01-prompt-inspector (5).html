How to play

You have just watched how a well-built prompt comes together. Now it is your turn to spot when one goes wrong.

  • 1
    Read the prompt in the chat window carefully.
  • 2
    Tap the part you think is the problem. There is exactly one flaw in each prompt.
  • 3
    If you are right, you will see the fix and a simulated AI response. If not, you will get a hint and can keep trying.
  • 4
    Work through all four rounds, one for each prompt element.

The four elements, fixed
1
Greet the AI
A greeting encourages you to write conversationally, which leads to a longer, more detailed prompt. The AI mirrors your register — more detail in means more useful detail out.
2
Provide context
Tell the AI what the resource is, who the learners are, and what you need.
3
Specify the output
Describe the format and end use so the AI shapes its output accordingly.
4
Give a clear instruction
Be explicit and leave no room for creative misinterpretation.
